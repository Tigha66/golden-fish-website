Our Story

Four Generations of Excellence

Golden Fish & Chips has been serving the East Ham community since 1985. What started as a small family shop has become a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to quality and authentic traditional recipes.

Our fish is delivered fresh daily from Billingsgate Market, and we only use the finest British potatoes for our legendary hand-cut chips. Every meal is cooked to order by our experienced team.
